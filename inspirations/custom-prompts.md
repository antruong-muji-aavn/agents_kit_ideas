# Custom Prompts — Ad-Hoc Research

One-off or exploratory research tasks. Run manually or schedule.

These are prompts that don't fit into the standing research directions. Use them for:
- Deep dives into specific problems
- Exploratory brainstorming
- Feedback-driven investigations
- Time-bounded research sprints

## Template

```
## Title

`prompt:<instructions>`

- When: <date created or "on-demand">
- Related to: <motivation or ticket>
- Status: pending|in-progress|completed
```
